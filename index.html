Projects summary

Balance Golf

The project brief provided by Balance golf, co developed by Esbjörn Ebbesson, described the problem area like the following: The website is not structured or adapted for the target group. It is neither designed in such a way that it explainscaptures our golfing concept as a whole. Initially the design team questioned this brief as the website as a whole seemed to be relatively well designed. By further examining the website with the project brief in mind the design team could uncover glaring issues in the informational architecture within the website. Therefore this became the focus of the project.

Region Halland

The briefs problems space encompassed Region halland and the bemanningsenheten. Today region hallands bemanningsenheten is in need of a more effective system for booking personnel who can drive and administer proper care inside of ambulances. This includes dividing work hours more efficiently and equally, seeing what competences different individuals have as well as having an efficient overview of work hours that are missing personnel and what competencies are needed inside the ambulance.

LBVA & VIVAB

LBVA and VIVAB wanted to make actors such as, private individuals and companies conscious of freshwater as a finite resource and as a prerequisite for all life to function normally. This involved making actors stop wasting freshwater on, as an example cleaning their cars.
